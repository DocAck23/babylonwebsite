Capabilities

From strategy to signed, shipped, and delivered.

Clients retain Babylon Consulting when an initiative is too large, too cross-border, or too politically sensitive for a generalist. Our capabilities compound—most engagements draw on several at once.

01 / Advisory

Strategic & Policy Advisory

Sector strategy, market entry, investment climate assessment, and policy-aware planning for heads of state, ministries, sovereign entities, and enterprise leadership. We produce decision-grade guidance—not decks to file.

02 / Origination

Deal Origination & Structuring

Sourcing, structuring, and negotiating mandates: concessions, joint ventures, offtake agreements, EPC packages, technology licenses, and cross-border commercial frameworks. We carry the deal from term sheet to close.

03 / Facilitation

Project Facilitation & Execution

The long arc from feasibility through financial close and commissioning. We orchestrate local partners, regulators, technical teams, and financiers—on the ground, in the room, for the duration of the mandate.

04 / Capital

Capital Introduction

Introductions to sovereign wealth, development finance institutions, export credit agencies, institutional investors, and discreet family-office capital—matched to mandate, jurisdiction, and risk profile.

05 / Trade

Import, Export & Trade Facilitation

Moving real goods across real borders: equipment, commodities, finished product. We architect the customs, compliance, freight, and bilateral framework so that signed contracts become delivered cargo.

06 / Procurement

Procurement & Equipment Sourcing

Turbines, heavy equipment, medical systems, industrial machinery. We source from Tier-1 OEMs and vetted intermediaries, vet specifications, negotiate price and terms, and manage delivery and commissioning.

07 / AI & Digital

AI & Digital Transformation Consulting

For governments and enterprises modernizing operations: AI strategy, workflow automation, data architecture, and the digital layer that modern institutions now require. Delivered with the same seriousness as our physical-economy mandates.

08 / Counterparty

Counterparty & Partner Introductions

Our value is often not the plan—it is the phone call. We maintain relationships across operators, contractors, ministries, and financiers on four continents, and we introduce with judgment.

How We Engage

A deliberate, four-stage arc.

We move at the pace the work demands—fast where speed creates advantage, patient where patience creates trust.

Stage 01

Dialogue

A private conversation to understand mandate, constraints, and counterparties. No deliverables, no presumptions.

Stage 02

Framing

A written engagement frame: scope, structure, success criteria, and the terms under which Babylon is retained.

Stage 03

Execution

The work itself—sustained, on-the-ground, and accountable to milestones we set jointly at the outset.

Stage 04

Hand-Over

A clean transition to the operators, partners, and institutions who will run the outcome for the long term.
